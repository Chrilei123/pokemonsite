Extra Starter Information

All the starters start at level 5, and they are always somewhat stronger than the pokemon you encounter in the beginning so that you have a headstart when you start your pokemon adventure. At the beginning of the game you choose between three types of starter Pokemon. The types you choose are Fire, Water and Grass. In every main pokemon game, you also face a rival that usually choose the starter pokemon which counters your starter pokemon. One could call it rock, paper and scissors, because how rock beats scissors, and scissors beat paper and paper beats rock, Fire beats Grass, Grass beats Water, and Water beats Fire.

The only case where this is not true is in Pokemon Sun, Moon, Ultra Sun and Ultra moon, where the rival always choose the pokemon that is weak to yours. Another thing to mention is the special case in Pokemon Yellow, which came after Red, Blue and Green, where you can only recieve Pikachu as your starter. In this game, you will also recieve all the first generation starters while playing through your adventure, and the way you recieve them is fairly similar to how Ash recieves them in the original tv series.
