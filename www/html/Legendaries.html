Legendary Pokemon

The legendaries of the pokemon games

  • Kanto Region | Red, Blue, Green, Yellow Game | Articuno, Zapdos, Moltres, Mew and Mewtwo
  • Johto Region | Gold, Silver, Crystal Game | Entei, Raikou, Suicune, Celebi, Lugia and Ho-oh
  • Hoenn Region | Ruby, Sapphire, Emerald Game | Regirock, Regice, Registeel, Kyogre, Latios, Latias, Groudon, Rayquaza and Deyoxys

  • Unova Region | Black, White, Black 2, White 2 Game | Cobalion, Virizion, Terrakion, Zekrom, Reshiram, Kyurem, Victini, Thundurus, Tornadus and Landorus
  • Kalos Region | X and Y Game | Xerneas, Yveltal and Zygarde
  • Alola Region | Sun and Moon Game | Solgaleo, Lunala, Necrozma, Cosmog, Tapu Koko, Tapu Lele, Tapu Bulu and Tapu Fini

How does it work?

In every pokemon game, there are something called Legendary Pokémon. These are extra powerful pokemon that you can only catch once, or twice depending on the legendary type. What is special them is that they are not encountered in grass, but rather as a sprite you can interact with. Here's a few examples of Legendary pokemon you can encounter.

Usually, if you defeat them once, you will not be able to find them again because they will only stay at the location you find them at first. Of course, this means that they will be harder to catch than normal pokemon you will find in grass, caves or at the ocean. Luckily, you can recieve something called a master ball which has a 100% catch rate, and is ment to use for these legendaries. There will exist more than one legendary in each game, so you have to be careful to use it, because like legendary pokemon, you will only recieve it one time. However, you can transfer it from other games by the trading system.
